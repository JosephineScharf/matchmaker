Lingner Castle

Perched above the Elbe River, Lingner Castle sits on a hillside overlooking the city and surrounding vineyards. The area combines historic architecture with open terraces and views across Dresden.

VISUAL VIBE

COLOUR THEME

Warm materials and evening light bring out golden, soft yellow, and warm stone tones, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

HIDDEN FOUNTAIN

The sound of flowing water creates a calm atmosphere, a places to pause and observe. A view metres away from an outstanding view.

WINERY

The historic vineyard culture with a relaxed outdoor setting. Visitors can sit among vines with views over the river landscape while enjoying local wine. Enjoy lovely wine and baked goods.

ROOFTOP

The rooftop terrace offers panoramic views over the Elbe valley and the city of Dresden. Its elevated position creates a sense of distance from the surroundings, making it a quiet spot for lingering and conversation.

Conversation starters

  • The castle was originally built in the 19th century and later owned by Karl August Lingner, a German entrepreneur and patron of social reform
  • The surrounding park is part of Dresden’s historic Elbe landscape and combines formal garden elements with open green spaces
